Hi, I'm Fiona!

I recently graduated from UC San Diego in March 2020. I majored in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Human-Computer Interaction, and minored in Psychology. I'm currently working part-time as a web designer / developer @PrecisePK and was previously a UX designer & researcher @UCSD Design Lab, as part of the Diabetes Design Initiative.

I am an empathetic UI/UX designer who likes to create meaningful user experiences that make people's lives a little bit easier. I have a user-centered design approach and want to design seamless user experiences across platforms.

I enjoy exploring new places and trying new boba places! In my free time, I also like to watch anime and Korean dramas. I've recently taken up baking and have been learning how to bake cookies, pizza, mochi muffins, pineapple buns, and more!
