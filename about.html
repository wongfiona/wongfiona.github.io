Hi, I'm Fiona!

I recently graduated from UC San Diego in March 2020. I majored in Cognitive Science with a specialization in Design & Interaction, and minored in Psychology. I'm currently working part-time as a web designer / developer @PrecisePK and was previously a UX researcher @UCSD Design Lab for the Diabetes Design Initiative.

I am interested in the intersections of technology and design. I enjoy learning new design tools and developing my skills while working on design projects.

My previous experiences include working with a business client, the president of Santorini Island Grill, to redesign their current mobile and desktop website. I also have industry experience from working with Dexcom, a company that develops products to help people manage diabetes. I conducted user research and helped redesign the user interface of their mobile app.
